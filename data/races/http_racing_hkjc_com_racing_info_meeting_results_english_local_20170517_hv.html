Racing Incident Report
GRACE HEART jumped out at the start, resulting in THE INVINCIBLE being hampered when tightened onto the hindquarters of FLYING GODSPELL. JOLLY BOUNTIFUL began only fairly. Soon after the start, FLYING GODSPELL and HIGH ENERGY raced tight inside STARTLING POWER which shifted in. Also soon after the start, SAKURA FIGHTER, which began awkwardly, had to be steadied when tightened for room between MORETHANLUCKY which shifted out and JUNZI which was taken in by TRENDIFUL. For the majority of the race, STARTLING POWER raced wide and without cover. A veterinary inspection of MORETHANLUCKY immediately following the race did not show any significant findings. GRACE HEART and OCEAN ROAR were sent for sampling.
